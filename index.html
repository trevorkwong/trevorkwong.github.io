Travel to White Rock

City by the sea!

Why is this place so great?

  • Great sunsets
  • Good food
  • Fishing/crabbing spot
  • Huge beach
  • Scenic

Things you can do!

Fishing

Buy a fishing license and walk down the longest pier! Cast your rod under the docks to fish. Drop a crab trap with bait and let it sit for ten minutes. Best of luck!

City Tour

Walk around the city and find many different stores. From shells to shirts, they sell it all! Later, stop by a fish and chips store and get a taste of the sea.

Beach Day

Run down to the beach with a shovel, bucket, and net. Play in the tide pools and enjoy catching and releasing all sorts of small critters like baby flounder, clams, snails, more. Build a sand castle!

Top 3 Foods found here!

  2. Gelato
  3. Seafood
